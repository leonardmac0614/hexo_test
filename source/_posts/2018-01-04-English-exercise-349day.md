"We may lay in a stock of pleasures, as we would lay in a stock of wine; but if we defer tasting them too long, we shall find that both are soured by age."
<p>储藏快乐，如同储藏美酒，若是耽搁太久才去品尝，就会发现它们早就变了质。</p>
